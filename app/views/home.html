Sit, Stay & Play!

Keeping you and your dog happy and healthy

We offer dog daycare, private pet sitting and grooming services. Our free-range daycare hosts a low number of dogs, so you can feel confident your pet is in a safer environment and not kenneled all day.

We also offer private pet sitting. This service is flexible: we play "dog nanny", offer taxi services, provide walks, and even stay overnight. Although most of our services are tailored for dogs, we also help with cats, rabbits and horses. We are happy to help with your exotic pet when you need a professional!
